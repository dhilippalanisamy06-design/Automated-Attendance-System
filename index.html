Problem Statement

Many rural schools still depend on paper attendance registers. Manual attendance consumes valuable classroom time, increases paperwork, creates human errors, and makes report generation difficult. This project provides a simple digital attendance management system that stores student data securely and generates reports automatically.

Objectives

Digital Attendance

Replace paper attendance with an easy digital solution.

Reports

Generate daily, weekly, and monthly attendance reports.

Save Time

Reduce paperwork and improve classroom efficiency.
